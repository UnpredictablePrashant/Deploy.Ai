How It Works

1. Sign Up

Register your account with Deploy.ai to start your journey towards seamless software deployment.

2. Confirm Your Email

Check your inbox for a confirmation email and verify your account to ensure full access to all features.

3. Interact with the AI Bot

Discuss your project requirements and preferences with our intelligent AI bot, designed to understand your needs.

4. Get an Instant Deployment Plan

Receive a customized deployment plan, a robust deployment architecture, and an instant quote. You have the option to start from scratch or build upon an existing production setup.

5. One Click Deploy

Initiate your deployment process with just a single click, sit back, and watch as Deploy.ai takes care of the rest.

6. Check Your Website or Application

Once the deployment is complete, review your website or application to ensure everything is running smoothly.

7. Make Changes and Redeploy!

If you need to make adjustments, Deploy.ai makes it easy to implement changes and redeploy swiftly.

8. Your Application is Ready!

Congratulations! Your application is now live and ready to serve your users. Deploy.ai ensures a hassle-free deployment experience.
